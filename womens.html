1. Natural Nails

Natural nails are your own nails shaped and maintained without any artificial enhancements. They can be polished or left bare for a clean, minimal look. Ideal for women who prefer low-maintenance beauty and healthy nail care.

2. Acrylic Nails

Acrylic nails are made by applying a liquid and powder mixture that hardens to form strong extensions. They're perfect for long-lasting designs and dramatic shapes like stiletto or coffin. Great for women who want durability and bold styles.

  • Longevity :

    2–3 weeks between fills; removal requires soaking in acetone or filing off.

  • Look & Feel:

    Very sturdy; can be filed into dramatic shapes (stiletto, coffin). Slightly thicker profile.

3. Gel Nails

Gel nails use a gel-based polish cured under a UV or LED light, offering a glossy, chip-resistant finish. They feel natural and flexible while lasting up to 3 weeks. A popular choice for busy women who want a sleek, polished look.

Dip Powder Nails

Dip nails involve dipping your nails into colored powder and sealing with a topcoat—no light needed. They offer strength and vibrant color, lasting longer than gel. Ideal for women who want durable, salon-quality nails.
