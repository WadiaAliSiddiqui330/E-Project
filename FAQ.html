FAQ's.

What is Nail Art?

Nail art is a creative way to decorate, paint, and enhance your nails using different designs, colors, and techniques.

How long does Nail Art last?

Depending on the type of polish and design (gel, acrylic, etc.), nail art can last anywhere from 1 to 3 weeks with proper care.

Do I need to book an Appointment?

Yes, we recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure availability.

Can I bring my own Nail Design Idea?

Absolutely! We love customizing nail art. Just show us a picture or describe what you want.

Are the Products used safe?

Yes, we use high-quality, non-toxic, and skin-safe products that are safe for most nail types.

How should I take care of my Nail Art?

Avoid harsh chemicals, moisturize your hands, and wear gloves while doing chores to extend the life of your nail art.

What types of Nail Art do you offer?

We offer gel art, acrylic nails, matte designs, rhinestone art, hand-painted designs, and more.

Do you offer home service?

Currently, we only offer services at our studio, but we may provide home services in the future.

How much does Nail Art cost?

Prices vary based on the design complexity and material.
